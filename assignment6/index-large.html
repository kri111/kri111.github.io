Using iOS Magnifier

List of challenges

  • At about 1:10 in the video, you can see that I placed the magnifier at the bottom of the screen. When I tried to move it back up to the top, I couldn't because my iPhone interpreted the gesture as trying to exit the app rather than understanding that I wanted to move the magnifier.
  • It's hard to type using the magnifier.
  • It's fairly difficult to move around within the magnifier using three fingers.

I used the magnifier without my glasses on (I have a very high prescription!) and overall I thought it was fairly useful and well designed.

Low Vision Mode

I made the contrast between colors in Low Vision Mode much higher by setting black backgrounds with white fonts or vice versa (in the case of an input box.) Also, the text size is much bigger and you can easily toggle between the two modes by using the toggle button at the top of the webpage.
